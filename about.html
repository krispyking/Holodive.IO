About HoloDive

We're building the world's first real-time AI dive mask — combining marine biology, computer vision, and wearable hardware to transform the underwater experience for every diver on the planet.

Our Mission

The ocean covers 71% of our planet, yet most of it remains unexplored and poorly understood. Every day, millions of divers swim through one of Earth's richest ecosystems — and return to the surface without knowing what they saw.

HoloDive changes that. By overlaying real-time AI intelligence directly into the diver's field of view, we make every dive informed, logged, and connected to global conservation efforts.

The Problem We're Solving

Today's dive computers give you depth and air time. They don't tell you that the fish you just saw is a critically endangered humphead wrasse. They don't log the coral bleaching you swam past. They don't connect your dive to the 50,000 researchers tracking reef health worldwide.

HoloDive does all of that — automatically, in real time, without taking your eyes off the reef.
