Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 June 2026

HoloDive ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for early access or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Diver type and product interest
  • Message content submitted via contact forms

When you use a HoloDive device (post-launch), additional data may be collected with your consent, including dive logs, GPS location, and video footage. Citizen science data contribution is always opt-in.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you product updates, early access information, and launch news
  • To respond to enquiries and support requests
  • To improve our product and understand our audience
  • To fulfil legal or regulatory obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. This involves cookies that track page views and interactions. You can opt out via your browser settings or the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@holodive.com.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice on our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries: privacy@holodive.com
