The Problem

Most divers encounter hundreds of species on every dive but can't identify them. Manual logging is slow and error-prone. Wrist computers give data, but no context. The ocean stays a mystery.

✨

The Solution

HoloDive overlays real-time species identification, behavior insights, and conservation data directly into your field of view. Every dive becomes informed, memorable, and meaningful.

What HoloDive Does

Your Dive Computer, Marine Biologist,
and Safety Buddy — Built Into Your Mask

🐠

Real-Time Species ID

AI identifies fish, coral, and sea life as you swim past — species name, behavior facts, and conservation status overlaid in your HUD.

📊

Automated Dive Log

Complete dive record generated automatically: species list, conditions, depth, duration, route, and annotated video. No slate required.

🛡️

Safety Buddy

Real-time depth, air time, no-deco limits, and buddy alerts in your field of vision — so you never need to break eye contact with the reef.

🌍

Citizen Science

Opt-in data contribution to global marine research databases. Your dives help track species populations and reef health worldwide.
